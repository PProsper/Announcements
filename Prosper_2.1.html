The Alchemist

    November 23, 2010
    by Paulo Coelho

REVIEW:


The Alchamist is a great read because it teaches the reader a great lesson in the values of honesty, integrity and faithfulness. The protagonists starts out from a small village giving up his simple live as a sheepheard to follow a signs that are based on faith in God. What the Protagonist comes to discover is that what he had been persuing had been with him all along. I recommend this book to anyone who is having a momentary struggle with understanding they're purpose and with seeing the grand scheme of things.
